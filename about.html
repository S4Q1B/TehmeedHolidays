About Tehmeed Holidays

Discover Kashmir with a local touch. Tehmeed Holidays specializes in creating unforgettable travel experiences, blending adventure, relaxation, and authentic Kashmiri hospitality.

Our Story

Founded in the heart of Kashmir, Tehmeed Holidays was born out of a passion to share the natural beauty, culture, and charm of this remarkable region. With years of experience, we offer tailored tours that capture the essence of Kashmir, from its majestic mountains to its serene valleys.

Our Mission

We believe in providing more than just a trip. Our mission is to craft journeys that connect our guests with Kashmir's true spirit—its people, traditions, and landscapes. We strive to offer experiences that are meaningful, sustainable, and unforgettable.

Why Choose Us?

  • Experienced local guides with deep knowledge of Kashmir
  • Personalized itineraries to suit every traveler's dream
  • Commitment to sustainable and responsible tourism
  • Exceptional customer service from start to finish
